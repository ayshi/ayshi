GOLDENBERG HOUSE BUILDING STUDY
During Media in the first semester of first year, students were assigned to groups of three. The group of three were then assigned a house designed by a famous architect to study as a precedent. In conjunction with the other members of my group, I studied the Goldenberg Residence by Louis Kahn. As part of the precedent study, I drew a plan, section, elevation, and paraline of the house.
